Since it first launched in 2007 Google Street View (GSV) has managed
to capture over sixteen million kilometres of Street View imagery across eighty-three countries.
This visualisation stands between a globe and a cartogram.
To visually represent how much or how little you can actually see by googling from Italy
a certain area of the world, more than a thousand Google Street View pictures have been taken
to fill the surfaces of the lands.
