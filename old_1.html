Fornasetti:
an eclectic artist

Esplosione / Ricostruzione | a mind's exploration.

A secret and almost inaccessible eclectic world composed of distant elements between them, apparently in contrast, made of ancient squares with fantastic palaces, monuments, arcades, and facades, inhabited by beautiful women, obelisks and pyramids.

Are all the debris and fragments of reality, trampled by Fornasetti, are used to rebuild his world as if it were a collage of memory, of experiences and inspirations.

Forced into our homes, we started to think of interiors, ways of living to objects, then also to the houses of others who, like us, has been in quarantine.

How do all the others live? What are the minimum requirements to talk about "home"? Are not houses also the rooms in which you create your own space of personalization, the prison cells, the rooms of the sick, the tent cities, the houses Furniture, nomadic homes, temporary homes? By answering these questions, we have created Nido.
