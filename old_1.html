Fornasetti:
an eclectic artist

Esplosione / Ricostruzione | a mind's exploration.

A secret and almost inaccessible eclectic world composed of distant elements between them, apparently in contrast, made of ancient squares with fantastic palaces, monuments, arcades, and facades, inhabited by beautiful women, obelisks and pyramids.

Are all the debris and fragments of reality, trampled by Fornasetti, are used to rebuild his world as if it were a collage of memory, of experiences and inspirations.

Fornasetti is first of all a collector.
The collector is in fact the only person able to appreciate that difference minimal, invisible to anyone else, in which consists all the importance, all the value of one object compared to another.
