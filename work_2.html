Powermama | Not just mamas, powermamas.

Powermama wants to offer a tailormade service, for a satisfying experience and from fast fruition. For this it was designed an algorithm that interprets actions and a set specific data, to derive preferences, the personality and situation of users and propose ad hoc contents and interactions.

Find mamas near you: we know the mothers and we know that they are all different. Our algorithm was designed to propose a social experience, adapting to each user and tracing evolutions over time. We thought creation tools for all needs: when you have your hands busy posting an audio will be a moment!



We want our moms to feel as safe as possible: your diary will never be public and the contents will have clear indications to choose whether to keep them privately or to share them with other users. We guarantee the authoritativeness of our sources, for informed and honest information. We have nothing to hide.
