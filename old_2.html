Stone heart | A museum about mountains, from the the body to the soul.

We tried to approach the personal dimension of those who frequent the peaks, discovering their infinite facets.

The archetype of the tree is employed to indicate an exhibition structure that grows upwards, but at the same time thins, reflecting the disposition of contents:
fully tangible and technical at the base and gradually more ephemeral and subjective going uphill.

Fornasetti is first of all a collector.
The collector is in fact the only person able to appreciate that difference minimal, invisible to anyone else, in which consists all the importance, all the value of one object compared to another.
