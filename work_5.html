The Tube Trap | An analysis of YT's content farms made for kids.

YouTube is one of the most used platforms to entertain kids, so popular that it overcame television and this does not necessarily happen under supervision.
Parents and babysitters give their children access to it, trusting its system and educational purposes. Left alone in front of the screen, children go through a great amount of videos, consuming anything recommended, as decided by YouTube’s algorithm.

Even considering just videos "for kids", a label that channels arbitrarily choose and that is not under strict control of YouTube, some peculiar aspects can be detected and this study aims to analyze them. Channels’ owners know exactly how its algorithm works and this also comes to match easily with what children like.

-
Which words have been used the most in the titles of top YouTube channels for kids from August to October 2020?

On YouTube, word-salads are a widespread phenomenon, but this is not the only method used to create appealing titles. We went through the list of top channels’ videos for kids. Looking at the titles of those channels, that own billions of views and followers and that earn millions of dollars per year, recurrences were counted. The result was reading the same lines thousands of times, even if constructed in different ways.
